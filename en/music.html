我愛你 / I love you

我愛你 / I love you or music at the crossroads of genres

Written and performed by Japanese band Cody・Lee(李), 我愛你 / I love you is a catchy 4:56 minute music released in 2020.

The music which, through a sung Japanese, makes us discover a touching declaration of love of a man to a woman who would be, according to the lyrics, ready to cross the world to be at the side of the latter, enumerating then numerous cities around the world.

The music which, through a sung Japanese, makes us discover a touching declaration of love of a man to a woman who would be, according to the lyrics, ready to cross the world to be at the side of the latter, enumerating then numerous cities around the world.

All in a style that picks up the standards of J-pop and those of alternative music, I love you stands as the single from her album 生活のニュース / life new.

With its very rhythmic and accentuated sounds, guided by the use of numerous auditory effects. The music offers a shifted dimension as well by the whimsical lyrics as by the electronic effects adding a real alternative aspect to this last one, then far from the precepts of the Japanese music known as "classic".

Originally considered a sub-genre of Japanese music, J-pop for Japanese pop is very strongly inspired by traditional Japanese music combined with the style of 1960s rock such as the beatles and the beach boys to name just two. Cody・Lee(李) is a group formed in 2018 and still active, with a cumulative 2 albums and 19 music, the still recent group stands out as an ideal representation of J-pop culture despite a still small community due to a lack of visibility ≈120'000 monthly listeners on spotify. Very active on social networks, the group shares a lot with its community which makes it all the more appreciated by fans. From my point of view, I really appreciate this music, discovered by chance while getting lost on a big streaming platform. Its energetic side helps me sometimes to overcome complicated periods.
